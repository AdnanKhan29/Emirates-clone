BEFORE YOU FLY

Baggage

Find out how much baggage you can take on board. You can also explore our helpful baggage services and find out how to fly with items like sports or mobility equipment.

Cabin baggage rules

Find out what you can bring on board as cabin baggage. The rules may have changed since your last flight and by destination so check the latest updates before you fly.


Checked baggage rules

You can pack more with our generous baggage allowance. Check the baggage rules for your travel class and route.


Delayed and damaged baggage

Sometimes checked-in baggage can be delayed or damaged in transit. See how we can help you find or replace your bags.


Unusual baggage or special allowance

If you're flying with items like sports equipment, musical instruments or mobility aids, you might be able to check in extra or oversized baggage on your flight.


A guide to dangerous goods

Some items can be dangerous to the safety of passengers or the aircraft. Find out the rules as well as specific regulations for the destination you're flying to.


Buy extra baggage allowance

If you want to take more with you, you can buy extra baggage allowance online at a discount, saving you time and money.
